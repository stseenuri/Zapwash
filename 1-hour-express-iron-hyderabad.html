Frequently Asked Questions

What are the time slot options?

We offer 4 express slots: 1-hour (max 10 pieces, +Rs.100), 3-hour (max 20 pieces, +Rs.100), 6-hour (max 40 pieces, +Rs.70), and 12-hour (unlimited, +Rs.50).

Is pickup and delivery free?

Yes! First-time customers get completely free pickup and delivery. For regular orders, free delivery on orders above Rs.200. Below that, Rs.30 delivery charge applies.

Can I book same-day service?

Yes! Book via WhatsApp and choose your preferred time slot. Most areas get pickup within 20-30 minutes during business hours (7 AM - 9 PM).

What if I exceed the piece limit?

For 1-hour slot (max 10), 3-hour (max 20), or 6-hour (max 40), please select the next slower slot if you have more pieces. 12-hour slot has no limit.

What fabrics can you iron?

We handle all fabric types - cotton, linen, silk, polyester, wool, rayon. Our team uses appropriate heat settings for each fabric to ensure safe results.

Do you iron sarees?

Yes! Plain sarees (Rs.40) and sarees with pleats/border work (Rs.60). We specialize in silk saree pressing with proper pleating.
