Dry Cleaning FAQs

What is dry cleaning and how is it different from washing?

Dry cleaning uses chemical solvents instead of water to clean delicate fabrics. It's ideal for materials like silk, wool, and suits that can shrink or get damaged with water washing.

Is dry cleaning safe for silk sarees?

Yes! Dry cleaning is the safest method for silk sarees, especially Kanjeevaram and Banarasi sarees. It preserves the fabric texture, color vibrancy, and zari work without damage.

How long does dry cleaning take?

Our standard dry cleaning service takes 72 hours (3 days). For urgent needs, we offer 48-hour express dry cleaning at 1.5x pricing. Available for most items.

Do you remove stains during dry cleaning?

Yes! Pre-treatment for stains like oil, wine, ink, and grease is included at no extra cost. We inform you if any stain cannot be completely removed before processing.

Can you dry clean leather jackets?

Absolutely. We specialize in leather and suede dry cleaning using specialized techniques that preserve the material's texture and appearance.

Is pickup and delivery free for dry cleaning?

Yes! First-time customers get completely free pickup and delivery. For regular orders, free delivery on orders above Rs.500. Below that, Rs.50 delivery charge applies.
