Frequently Asked Questions

What is dry cleaning?

Dry cleaning uses chemical solvents instead of water to clean delicate fabrics. Safe for silk, wool, suits, and designer wear that can't be machine washed.

How long does dry cleaning take?

Standard turnaround is 72 hours (3 days). Pickup today = delivery in 3 days. Rush service available for urgent needs at additional charge.

Can you remove all types of stains?

We successfully remove 95% of stains. Oil, wine, ink, grease, food - most come out. Some old or set stains may lighten but not fully disappear.

Is dry cleaning safe for silk sarees?

Yes! Dry cleaning is the safest method for silk sarees. Maintains color, sheen, and fabric quality. We specialize in wedding and designer sarees.

Do you dry clean wedding wear?

Absolutely! We handle sherwanis, wedding sarees, designer lehengas, bridal gowns. Expert care for expensive and delicate wedding outfits.

Is pickup and delivery free?

Yes! First order completely free. Regular orders: free delivery above Rs.200. Below that, Rs.30 charge applies.
