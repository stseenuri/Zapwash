Frequently Asked Questions

Who is iron-only service for?

Perfect for people who wash clothes at home (in washing machine or by hand) but want professional, crisp ironing. Save money while getting wrinkle-free results.

How much does iron-only cost?

Starting at Rs.10/piece for shirts. Trousers Rs.15, Kurtas Rs.20, Plain sarees Rs.30. Most affordable ironing service in Hyderabad!

How fast is the turnaround?

24 hours! Pickup today = delivery tomorrow. Much faster than wash & iron because we skip the washing step.

Do clothes need to be completely dry?

Yes! Clothes must be washed and fully dried before pickup. We only iron, we don't dry clothes. Make sure they're 100% dry.

Is pickup and delivery free?

Yes! First-time customers get completely free pickup and delivery. Regular orders: free above Rs.200. Below that, Rs.30 charge.

Can I get same-day service?

Not for regular iron-only. But check our Express Iron service for 1-hour, 3-hour, 6-hour, and 12-hour delivery options!
