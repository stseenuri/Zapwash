Iron Only Service FAQs

What is iron only service?

Iron only service is professional steam pressing for clothes that are already clean. Perfect for clothes you've washed at home but need a professional crisp press.

How long does iron only service take?

Standard turnaround is 24 hours. Drop off today, get delivery tomorrow. For bulk orders (50+ pieces), please allow 48 hours.

Is pickup and delivery really free?

Yes! First-time customers get completely free pickup and delivery. For regular orders, free delivery on orders above Rs.200. Below that, Rs.30 delivery charge applies.

Do you iron sarees?

Yes! We iron all types of sarees - cotton, silk, synthetic. Plain sarees at Rs.40, sarees with pleating at Rs.60. Pleating done according to your preference.

What if my clothes have stains?

Iron only service is for clean clothes. If your clothes have stains or need washing, please book our Wash & Iron service instead for complete cleaning.

Can I iron bed sheets and home linen?

Absolutely! We iron bed sheets (Rs.30 single, Rs.50 double), pillow covers, curtains, and all home linen items.
